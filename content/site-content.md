## About Page

### Organization Overview
The GRACE Cancer Foundation is an India-based nonprofit dedicated to combating cancer through five core areas: "Education, Early detection, Treatment, Rehabilitation, and cutting-edge Research." The organization offers "CURE, CARE, and COMPASSION" as its guiding principles.

### Mission
The foundation commits to improving quality of life for cancer patients, advancing research, and promoting early detection while fostering community strength and compassion.

### Vision
To ensure every cancer patient receives optimal care and emotional support, working toward minimizing cancer's impact.

### Founding Story
The organization emerged from a dedicated group of doctors extending their work beyond professional duties. Many founders drew inspiration from personal experiences with cancer. Dr. Chinnababu's mother inspired the mobile screening campaign, demonstrating how shared suffering unites people toward purpose.

The foundation evolved from an NGO to a social enterprise, gaining 12A and 80G tax exemptions in 2014 and FCRA registration in 2018 for receiving foreign donations.

### Key Milestones (2013-2025)
- **2013:** Inaugurated
- **2015:** Established first mobile screening bus
- Secured multiple **Guinness World Records** for breast, cervical, and oral cancer awareness campaigns
- Expanded to **6 mobile screening buses**
- Launched **Global Grace Cancer Run** (now reaching 150,000+ participants)
- Advanced digital screening platforms
- Established ICU facilities
- Implemented HPV vaccination and tribal cancer screening programs

## Education & Awareness Page

### Heading
Education & Awareness

### Content
"At Grace Cancer Foundation, we are dedicated to making a difference in the fight against cancer. Our mission is to provide education and raise awareness about cancer to empower individuals and communities to take control of their health and well-being."

"Knowledge is a powerful tool in the battle against cancer." The foundation commits to sharing vital information, resources, and support.

### Case Study: Yashoda's Story
Yashoda Perala was diagnosed with cervical cancer in early 2013. Despite lacking financial means and knowledge about treatment options, the foundation conducted a free health camp in her village in Nalgonda district, Telangana State. GCF identified an appropriate cancer center, initiated her chemotherapy, and provided necessary medications. At age 50, Yashoda experienced relief from both physical and emotional suffering and expressed gratitude for the timely intervention.

## Early Detection Page

### Heading
Early Detection

### Key Program: Mobile Cancer Screening Bus
The foundation operates a mobile cancer screening initiative that brings diagnostic services to underserved populations. "State Bank of India donated a Mobile Cancer Screening Bus" to facilitate this outreach work. The program was inspired by Dr. Chinnababu's desire to honor his mother's legacy of serving disadvantaged communities.

### Current Operations
The foundation maintains **four fully equipped mobile screening units** providing cancer detection services. They have "conducted over **850 events** including Awareness talks, Mobile Cancer Screening Camps, Free Surgical Oncology and Medical Oncology camps."

### Key Challenges Identified
- Lack of awareness and ignorance about cancer
- Limited access and reach to screening services
- Low priority for preventive healthcare
- Lifestyle factors including tobacco and alcohol use

## Treatment & Rehabilitation Page

### Heading
Treatment & Rehabilitation

### Content
"The Grace Cancer Foundation is dedicated to providing compassionate care and support to cancer patients through its Treatment & Rehabilitation Free Medical Camps."

The foundation organizes camps offering essential medical services at no cost to individuals battling cancer. Financial barriers should not prevent patients from accessing quality care during their recovery journey.

### Services Provided at Medical Camps
- Medical treatments (chemotherapy, radiation therapy, surgeries)
- Psychological support and counseling
- Nutritional guidance
- Physical therapy and rehabilitation services

## Cutting Edge Research Page

### Heading
Cutting Edge Research / Cancer Research

### ClinSync Connection
ClinSync operates as the umbrella organization for Grace Cancer Foundation. The foundation emerged from research conducted at ClinSync.

### Facility Details
- "State of Art facilities" across a **50,000 sq.ft** sprawling facility
- 6-bedded ICU maintained per American Society of Cardiology guidelines
- Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support certified intensivists
- Described as "one of the first kinds in India"

### Service Areas & Expertise
- Protocol development
- Phase-I, BA/BE clinical studies
- Data management and quality operations
- Data monitoring and medical monitoring
- Phase-III/IV and epidemiological trials
- Project management

### Therapeutic Specializations
Oncology, Nephrology, Neurology, Cardiovascular, Endocrinology, Psychiatry, Geriatric, and Pulmonary Medicine.

### Vision
"Be a preferred and globally respected research partner" providing services emphasizing "honesty, quality and compliance."
